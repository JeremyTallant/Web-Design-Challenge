Cloudiness

There is no strong correlation between cloudiness and latitude. However, there are some factors that can influence both. For example, cities near the equator tend to have more stable weather patterns, which can lead to fewer clouds and more clear skies. In contrast, cities further away from the equator tend to have more variable weather patterns, which can lead to more clouds and less clear skies. Additionally, factors such as altitude, the presence of mountains, and local weather patterns can also influence cloudiness in a given location.
