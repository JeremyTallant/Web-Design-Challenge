Humidity

While there seems to be no strong correlation between humidity and latitude, there are some indirect factors that can influence both. For example, as you move closer to the equator, the climate tends to be warmer, which can lead to higher levels of humidity. This is because warm air can hold more water vapor than cold air. Additionally, areas near the equator tend to receive more sunlight and rainfall, which can also contribute to higher levels of humidity.
